Welcome to Sipna C.O.E.T, Amravati

Sipna Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, established in 1995, aims to impart higher & technical education to enthusiastic youths and envision them for a better tomorrow.

Since its inception, SSPM has been successfully running undergraduate and postgraduate courses in emerging areas through Sipna’s Arts, Science & Commerce college, Chikhaldara, and Sipna College of Engineering & Technology, Amravati, affiliated with Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University.

Vision

To provide quality professional education and a conducive environment for students to emerge as a model proficient institute.

Mission

To create a scholarly and vibrant environment for professional excellence and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in basic and applied areas of engineering and technology.
